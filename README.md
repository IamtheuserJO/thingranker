# Thingranker
This a website where you can rank things. <b>
<b>
<bold> How does it work?</bold><b>
I will explain this with a step-to-step instruction:

<li>
  <ol>You input the name of the things you want to rank.</ol>
  <ol>You get a question where you choose the best of two alternatives. The alternatives are chosen from your inputs.</ol>
  <ol>You repeat step 2 until you have done does with all combinations.</ol>
  <ol>When you choose the best of the two alternatives, the best alternative scores a point. The total score is automatically calculated.</ol>
  <ol>An list that ranks all inputs after their score gets outputed for you to see.</ol>
</li>
