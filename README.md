# Thingranker
This a website where you can rank things. <br>
<br>
<b> How does it work?</b> </br>
I will explain this with a step-to-step instruction:

<ol>
  <li>You input the name of the things you want to rank.</li>
  <li>You get a question where you choose the best of two alternatives. The alternatives are chosen from your inputs.</li>
  <li>You repeat step 2 until you have done does with all combinations.</li>
  <li>When you choose the best of the two alternatives, the best alternative scores a point. The total score is automatically calculated.</li>
  <li>An list that ranks all inputs after their score gets outputed for you to see.</li>
<ol>
